Notes for review
Concept A — Recommended primary direction. Evolves the current mark (retaining brand equity), introduces indigo accent, reads clearly at all sizes. The indigo rule beneath the icon is the brand differentiator.

Concept B — Most abstract and versatile. Works well as a standalone icon on LinkedIn or as an embossed mark on print collateral. More distinctive at small sizes but less immediately legible as an S.

Concept C — Most formal. The tracked uppercase wordmark and gold crosshair give it gravitas for printed materials. The crosshair is a strong conceptual hook (precision, targeting, analysis) but may read as a pharmacy cross at very small sizes.

Recommendation: Use Concept A as the primary nav/web mark, Concept B as the standalone icon/app mark for LinkedIn/favicon, and consider Concept C for future print collateral and letterhead. This hybrid approach preserves the best of all three.
